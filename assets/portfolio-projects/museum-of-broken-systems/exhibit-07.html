Silence

In the quiet spaces between the noise,
we find the thoughts we lost.


The screen is a window,
not a wall.


But when the window is covered
in painted signs,


We forget there is a world
outside the frame.


We click, we scroll,
we accept.


And silence becomes
the most expensive luxury.


End.
